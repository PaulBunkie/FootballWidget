# BetON — Rock-Solid Bets

Stop tracking matches — BetON will do it for you.

The app automatically monitors football matches with a clear favorite and notifies you only when a truly important event occurs.

## Key Feature — "Favorite in Trouble"

Hundreds of football matches take place every day, and it's impossible to track them all. BetON does it for you.

The app automatically tracks matches where there is a clear favorite. As long as everything goes according to the expected scenario, it doesn't distract you. But if the favorite suddenly starts losing or loses their advantage, BetON instantly sends a **"Favorite in Trouble"** notification.

Open the match with a single tap, evaluate the situation on the field using the attack momentum chart and other live metrics, and decide for yourself whether this situation deserves your attention.

BetON does not give predictions or tell you what to do. It simply helps you not to miss the moment when the usual course of the game changes.

## Features

⚽ **Live Widget** with current and upcoming matches right on your home screen.

⭐ **Favorite Strength Adjustment** — choose how clear the team's advantage should be for the match to appear in your tracking list.

🎨 **Widget Personalization** — adjust the widget background transparency directly in the app for a perfect match with your wallpaper.

🔔 **Instant Notifications**

* match start;
* goals scored;
* **"Favorite in Trouble"** — when the favorite starts losing or loses their lead;
* match end.

📊 **Quick Access to Match Info** — open detailed information, attack charts, and other live indicators with one click to assess the situation.

⚡ **Instant Updates** via Firebase Cloud Messaging without constant server polling.

🔋 **Energy Efficient** — the app doesn't drain your battery with unnecessary background requests.

🌍 **Multi-language Support**: Russian, English, Ukrainian, Belarusian, Kazakh, Spanish, French, German, and Chinese.

BetON is not trying to replace a sports portal or a betting app. It takes over the most tedious work — monitoring matches and reporting only when something truly important happens.
